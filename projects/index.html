Projects

I have been involved in various projects, as a Principal Investigator (PI), Co-Investigator (Co-I), and lead researcher. A selection of these is listed below. If you are interested in any of these project, especially current ones, please do contact me! :-)

TIPS in blended home-work environments post-COVID: A socio-technical perspective
Project funded by EPSRC and the The Security, Privacy, Identity and Trust Engagement NetworkPlus (SPRITE+ HUB). More info here. (2020-2021)

Incentivising Cybersecurity through Cyber Insurance (ICCI)

Project funded by the National Cyber Security Centre(NCSC)/Research Institute for Sociotechnical Cyber Security(RISCS), and in partnership with Royal United Services Institute (RUSI). More info here. (2020-2021)

Supporting Home Users Engagement with Cyber Security

Project funded by HMG. (2020-2022)

A Platform for Responsive Conversational Agents to Enhance Engagement and Disclosure (PRoCEED)
Project funded by the EPSRC, in collaboration with University of East Anglia, Cranfield University and Oxford Brookes University, and six other industry and government partners. More info here. (2019-2021)

Analysing Cyber Value-at-Risk

Project funded by industry. More info here. (2019)

Cyber Risk Assessment for Coupled Systems
Project funded by UKRI and it was a part of the PETRAS National Centre of Excellence. This specific project was in partnership with Fujitsu Labs Europe and NSC. More info here. (2016-2018)

Prioritising open-source social media information: separating fact from fiction

Project funded by Dstl CDE, in collaboration with University of Reading and CountingLab. (2016-2017)

Proactive Risk Management through Improved Situational Awareness (PROTECTIVE)

Project funded by the European Union’s Horizon 2020 Research and Innovation Programme, in collaboration with Athlone Institute of Technology, Technische Universität Darmstadt and seven other partners. More info here. (2016-2019)

An Investigation into the Effectiveness of Security Risk Controls in Organisations

Project funded by industry. (2016)

Corporate Insider Threat Detection: Cyber Security Inside and Out (CITD)

Project funded by HMG. More info here. (2013-2015)

Trust-Enabling Augmented-reality Support for information Environments (TEASE)
Project funded by Innovate UK. More info here. (2011-2013)
